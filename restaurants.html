Golden Dragon

GOLDEN DRAGON is a restaurant which serves dishes from Chinese cuisine.
China has one of the most influential culinary traditions in the world with a diverse selection of food. We offer a wide range of dishes often showcase the different regional flavors and cooking techniques from different parts of China.
We in Golden Dragon is known for our balance in flavors, including sweet, sour, salty, bitter and its use of fresh ingredients, aromatic spices and variety of cooking methods.

Masala House

MASALA HOUSE is a restaurant which serves dishes from Indian cuisine.
We offer a rich and diverse culinary experience, showcasing the variety of flavours, aromas, and spices which are unique to indian foods.
We typically serve a wide variety of dishes from different regions of india ranging from shorteats like samosas, to naan, curry and to more contemporary fusion foods.

Thai Food Hub

THAI FOOD HUB is a restaurantwhich serves dishes from Thai cusine.
We offer a delightful and exotic culinary journey into the vibrant flavours Thai cusine.
We present diverse range of dishes, from classic dishes like Pad Thai, Tom Yum Goong(spicy shrimp soup) to lesser-known regional dishes.
We tend to offer a tantalizing exploration of a cusine known for its harmoniooous blend of ingredients and flavours.
