ABOUT US


Our story

Hotel Castle has become a symbol of luxurious escapism over the past decade.
We have hosted glorious weddings, romantic getaways which made couples speechless by the enchanting surroundings and many fun activities that leave our guests mind blown.
We have 3 branches around Sri Lanka each with its own unique personality.
We are always bounded to provided the best customer service despite the challanges we face.



Hotel experience

Stepping through the elegant entrance of the Hotel Castle, Our customers will be greeted by the receptionist with a lovely welcome drink.
Hotel lobby is decorated with modern artwork, plush seating and soft ambient lighting. Upon entering the room, captivated by the soothing atmosphere of soft melody of music echoning through the air.
Each room was tastefully decorated in a soothing color and with unique cozy seatings and bed adorned with crisp linen and plump pillows.
Hotel Castle has a state of the art entertainment system, offering a selection of movies and shows for a quiet night in.
Comprised of an open kitchen and a menu that showcase three different cultural flavours,promising a culinary adventure.
Attention to every detail was evident, From the attentive housekeeping team to the courteous waitstaff, everyone seemed dedicated to making an exceptional experience.
